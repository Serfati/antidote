About

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus. At this time, we are in front of vaccines or treatments for COVID-19 and waiting to stop the spread. The best way to prevent and slow down transmission is be well informed about the COVID-19 virus.(WHO).

The goal of this project is not to build just another dashboard. But, to focus on collaborative plot ideas, and a friendly UI/UX
